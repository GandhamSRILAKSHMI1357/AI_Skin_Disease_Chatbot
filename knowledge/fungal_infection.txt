Fungal skin infections are caused by fungi growing on the skin.

Symptoms:
- Itching
- Red circular rash
- Peeling skin
- Burning sensation

Common Types:
- Ringworm
- Athlete's foot
- Jock itch

Treatment:
- Antifungal creams
- Antifungal tablets prescribed by a doctor
- Keep skin dry

Prevention:
- Maintain hygiene
- Avoid sharing towels
- Wear clean clothes
