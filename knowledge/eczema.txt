Eczema, also called atopic dermatitis, is a condition that makes the skin dry, itchy, and inflamed.

Symptoms:
- Dry skin
- Severe itching
- Red patches
- Cracked skin

Causes:
- Allergies
- Genetics
- Irritants
- Dry weather

Treatment:
- Moisturizers
- Steroid creams
- Antihistamines
- Avoid allergens

Prevention:
- Keep skin moisturized
- Avoid harsh soaps
- Wear soft cotton clothing
